Game Rules: üìÉ

This game is a turn-based strategy game. Each player rolls on to one of five characters: Dragon, Nymph, Shield, Sword, or Trap.
Here are the rules for the game:

  • Dragon DEFEATS Shield & Trap
  • Nymph DEFEATS Dragon & Sword
  • Shield DEFEATS Nymph & Sword
  • Sword DEFEATS Dragon & Trap
  • Trap DEFEATS Nymph & Shield

May you get your roll character to gain an advantage over your opponent!ü§†

Gameplay: üéÆ

The game starts with a toss to determine which player goes first. The player with the winning toss gets to make the first move.
Players can choose to Attack or Heal on their turn:

  • Attack: Costs 5 credits and deals 2 damage to the opponent's health.
  • Heal: Costs 3 credits and restores 1 point of health.

Keep an eye on your credits and health to make strategic decisions.ü§î
